Faq

How can I register?

To register at disabilityjobs.gov.in, click on the ‘Job Seeker Sign up’ or ‘Register Now’ option on www.disabilityjobs.gov.in. This takes you to the next step of filling up your personal and professional details. These details are important information that the recruiters look for. Once you’ve completed this, your profile is registered with us. This is your default profile which is sent across the recruiters when you apply for a particular job vacancy. We highly recommend you to fill all details.

How can I update/edit my Profile?

You can update your profile only after you login. To edit your profile, click on the ‘My Profile ’ link. You can make changes to your Profile by editing the relevant sections. It is recommended that you update your Profile regularly. Recruiters often contact only those candidates who have recently updated their profiles.

How can I change the password to my account?

To change your account password, click on the ‘Change Password’ link as shown in the screenshot below. You would need to enter your old password and specify and confirm the new one.

Who all have access to my resume?

Once you have registered with complete details, all the recruiters who are registered with www.disabilityjobs.gov.in can access it.

How can I apply for a selected job?

Once you have selected the job/s that you want to apply for, there are two types of Apply options available to you: Single Apply.With Single Apply,you can apply for one job at a time. Multiple Apply,With Multiple Apply,you can apply for more than one job at a go. All you have to do is to select the jobs you want to apply for.

Can I apply for more than one job at a time?

Yes, you have an option to apply for more than one job at a time. Using the Multiple apply option

How do I know if I have successfully applied for a job?

In case of a successful job application, you will see the job applied list by selecting job applied option.

How do I search for relevant jobs?

Search gives you an option to look for relevant jobs based on your specific requirements. To search for a job, you can use the given search window. It is recommended that you specify the keywords, preferred locations and your total experience criteria to access job search results which are closest to what you are looking for.

How can I post a job.

a) Login to your Recruiter’s account at www.disabilityjobs.gov.in with the username and password mailed to you earlier. Go to "Publish Job" » Post a Job b) Fill the Job Publishing form, preview and post it.

For how long will my posted jobs remain advertised the site?

All jobs remain active on the site depending upon the date provided in the section ‘ Job Live Date ‘ and ‘Job Expiry Date’.
