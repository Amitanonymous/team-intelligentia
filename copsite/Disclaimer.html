Disclaimer

   
 

The job portal for PwDs is designed to provide free-of-charge service to the qualified candidates as well as to the Training Institutes ,NGO and other employers with an aim to help the PwD candidates to get suitable jobs. It is also a platform for employers, Training Institutes and other organisation to get qualified candidates. Hiring and monitory compensation for work performed by the candidates is handled directly between the candidate and the employer.

The Department of Empowerment of Person with Disabilities (DEPwD) does not perform background checks on candidates applying for the jobs, nor on employers posting job opportunities through this portal.

Employers and candidates are encouraged to request authentication and reference information from each other as required to establish qualifications and credentials claimed by the candidates and to establish overall fit between the employer and the applicant.

The DEPwD, through this portal, provides a referral service for the benefit of Persons with Disabilities in India and makes no particular recommendations regarding employers. The DEPwD does not make any representations or guarantees about positions posted by the employers. The DEPwD is not responsible for safety, wages, working conditions, or any other aspect of employment. Candidates are urged to perform due diligence in researching employers when applying for or accepting employment. While jobs posted on our website are intended to reach out to the qualified candidates, employers must ensure that the job advertisements should reach out to the general public.

We request the employers, training providers & other organizations to please check the disability certificate of the candidates before offering any opportunity.
